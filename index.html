Education in Australia

This page shows an overview on how educated Australians are.

Education enrollment by state

The map here shows how many people in each state are currently enrolled in studies. Use the filter at the bottom of the map to check how it changes over time. This number includes all student enrollments from Year 10 to Postgraduate diplomas and degrees. To see the breakdown of how many students pursuing each course, check the bar chart at the bottom.

Enrollment changes over time

The line chart on the left allows further visualisation of how student enrollment has changed over time. The isotype below shows the breakdown of the population in each state, with domestic students in blue, international students in green, and all other population in grey.

Who are these students

The following charts on the right show demographics of the students in Australia. The stacked bar chart shows the breakdown of what educational qualifications people in each state have.
