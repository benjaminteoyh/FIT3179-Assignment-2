Education in Australia

How educated are Australians?

Education Enrollment by State

In Australia, the state with the most students enrolled in study is New South Wales with a total of 1,006,800 students. Victoria closely follows, with a total of 828,000 students in total in 2023. This is consistent throughout the last 10 years. On the other hand, the state with the least students is the Northen Territory with a total of 29,400 students.

Changes in Enrollment Over Time

The rankings of states in terms of total student enrolment is consistently:

1. New South Wales

2. Victoria

3. Queensland

The only states with similar enrollment figures are Tasmania & Australian Capital Territory.

Highest Education Attained

The most common qualification that Australian's hold is a Bachelor Degree, with over 3.9 million Australians holding one. However, this is largely skewed due to the number of Bachelor holders in NSW & VIC. Excluding NSW, VIC, & ACT, Certificate III/IV is actually the most common qualification held.
